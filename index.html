Conor McGregor vs Khabib Nurmagomedov, UFC 229: betting tips and best odds

Conor McGregor, on hand there to clarify that this weekend’s pay-per-view subscribers will indeed be patched through to the 229th renewal of the Ultimate Fighting Championship live from Las Vegas, not a vicars’ tea party. McGregor certainly knows how to sell the sizzle, but it’s the sausage he should be wary of as for the first time in his UFC career (and ignoring his recent foray into boxing) the bookmakers have him down as an underdog, with the odds suggesting Khabib Nurmagomedov from Dagestan will be by far his toughest opponent to date. The bookies don’t usually miss a trick and they too must have seen that video of him wrestling a bear for fun as a child.
